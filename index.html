Fort Lauderdale’s newest yacht marine service facility

Announcing Port 32 Fort Lauderdale, a new in-water yacht facility that will offer storage, repair and retrofits for vessels up to 150 feet. Port 32 Fort Lauderdale is located west of I-95 on the New River, just north of Marina Mile. With convenient proximity to both the
Fort Lauderdale (7 miles) and Miami (30 miles) international airports, Port 32 Fort Lauderdale will offer competitive yachting services.

Featured Future Services Include:

  • Captain’s/Crew Lounge with TV, Wi-Fi, laundry, shower facilities, and entertainment plus grilling area
  • The only marine fuel dock on the New River
  • Concierge services
  • Tender hauling, service, storage, and repair up to
    55 feet in length
  • Multi-purpose storage units
  • Yacht service vendors to address all repair/retrofit needs

Phase I preleasing to begin May 2018 with slips available Summer 2018
